Greater Opportunities in Africa

The 100 Best Companies Work in Africa:Up to now, the recruitment narrative has too often focused on telling the story from the employer side.

What had not been provided was a view from the African talent pool itself. This is what we have set out to achieve in what is the most comprehensive and complete study to be done in Africa to date: the Careers in Africa Employer of Choice study.In total, 13,242 African professionals provided their views on employer attractiveness to help us put together a ranking of the continent’s top employers. The survey, methodology and outreach was spearheaded by leading recruitment specialists the Global Careers Company in association with Towers Watson.
